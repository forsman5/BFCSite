Welcome!

Welcome to the Buckeye Fencing Club Website! The BFC meets at The Ohio State University, in Multipuropose Room 3 on the second floor of the Recreation and Physical Activity Center (RPAC), 337 W. 17th Avenue, Columbus, OH 43210 (see side panel for times).

The mission of the BFC is to enhance and promote the element of good sportsmanship, the development of skills, the well-being derived from physical activity, and the social aspect within the sport. We hope you enjoy your stay with the Buckeye Fencing Club!
