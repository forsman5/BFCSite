Club Information

Everyone is welcome, including people with absolutely no fencing experience, people who have been fencing for years, freshman to grad students, and more, just stop by the next meeting to check it out! No cost to try it out, and all equipment is provided - just wear shorts or sweatpants and athletic shoes.

Practice Information

When: Mondays from 9:00 to 11:00pm, Thursdays from 8:30 to 10:30pm.
Where: RPAC Multipurpose Room 3

Note: No practice will be held on days where no classes are held (such as holidays). If practice is cancelled for any other reason, then all members will be notified by email, as well as posted on Facebook.
